Welcome!

I am a PhD scholar (expected to graduate in 2026) at the Indian Institute of Technology, Goa, working under the guidance of Dr. Neha Karanjkar. My research focuses on Simulation Modelling and Digital Twins. I am currently developing an open-source Python-based framework specifically designed for modeling and simulating supply chain systems. The tool supports configurable components such as retailers, warehouses, factories, and demand nodes, which can be connected to model and simulate custom supply chain networks. Users can simulate these networks for performance analysis and optimization.

Research Interests: Discrete Event Simulation, Digital Twins, Supply Chain Management, Operations Research, and Simulation-based Optimization.
